My name is Alethia Li. Alethia comes from the Greek word aletheia, which means truth. It’s also the name of the Greek goddess of truth. Fun things that come with this name: I haven’t met or heard of anyone with the same name as me. And people can surprise me with the pronunciation and spelling. Whenever I order food there’s a moment of hesitation before I say my name as clearly as possible. It’s always nice when they get it right. Once there was someone who remembered me from the day before, which was really nice.

You are what you love

My list of hobbies has grown throughout the years, but usually the first thing I think of is reading. I love reading books and the power that words can have, and the art of storytelling. I also dabble in storytelling myself, writing when I can. Finally, musical theatre brings everything else together— my hobbies of singing, playing piano and dancing. Acting is a new one, but I do love it. Also I do like to draw. You might be able to tell, I am a very arts oriented person.

My future

Being a junior in high school, it has become a bigger thing in my life to know what I want to do in the future. When I was in elementary school I had a dream of being a professional ballet dancer. Then I thought maybe I would be an artist? An author? In high school now it’s less easy to throw around ideas. There’s always the fear that there isn’t a way to make a living with a degree in whatever. And maybe I should just go for what I want, but there are people in my life who think otherwise. So that is a problem I’m having.
